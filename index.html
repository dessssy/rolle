About the App
How money works?
Financial Pilots

This app teaches you about money and investments. It teaches by simulating a life with financial ups and downs. It teaches you the language of business: Balance Sheet, Income Statement and Cashflow Statement. And it teaches you how to handle tough financial situations like pilots are taught in flight simulators.

Money has value only because people agree to give it value. It is a medium of exchange, and a way to store value. Currency and financial accounts might not have any value on their own, but money becomes valuable when everybody agrees to use it.
